FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is DishCovery? ▼
DishCovery is an interactive guide that helps you decide what to eat, whether you're cooking at home or choosing where to eat out.
Is it free to use? ▼
Yes, DishCovery is completely free to use.
How does DishCovery help me decide what to eat? ▼
It uses a food randomizer, filters, and recipe suggestions to help you explore options based on your cravings or preferences.
Do I need to create an account to use DishCovery? ▼
No account is required to use the basic features.
How does the food randomizer work? ▼
The randomizer selects a food or cuisine option based on your selected filters like type (home-cooked or fast food) and category.
Does DishCovery provide recipes? ▼
Yes, if you choose to cook, the system suggests a recipe complete with ingredients and preparation steps.
Is my data safe and private? ▼
Yes. We prioritize privacy and do not share user data with third parties.
What personal information does DishCovery collect? ▼
DishCovery only collects minimal usage data to improve the app. No personal info is required to use it.
Can I tell DishCovery to avoid certain foods? ▼
Yes! You can specify if you want to avoid fried food, sugar, dairy, red meat, seafood, or anything else you prefer not to eat.
